Project 1 - Dolphin Presentation

Dolphins are some of the most fascinating creatures on the planet, blending high-level intelligence, complex social structures, and remarkable evolutionary adaptations. Here is a deep dive into the extraordinary abilities and characteristics of the dolphin species:

  • Advanced Sensory Abilities: Dolphins experience the world through a highly specialized set of senses, the most famous of which is their built-in sonar.
  • Echolocation: Dolphins emit high-frequency clicks through their nasal passages, focused by a fatty organ in their forehead called the melon. The sound waves bounce off objects and return to the dolphin’s lower jaw, allowing them to "see" a three-dimensional acoustic image of their environment—even in pitch-black or murky water.
  • Vision: They have excellent vision both above and below the water's surface. Their eyes can adjust to varying light levels and pressures.
  • Hearing: Their hearing is incredibly acute. They can detect frequencies well outside the range of human hearing, using it for both navigation and communication.

Project 2 - Mountain Exploration
